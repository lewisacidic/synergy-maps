Synergy Maps

Rich Lewis 2015

Synergy Maps is a visualization tool for pairwise compound combination datasets.

It was developed by Rich Lewis, a PhD student in the Bender Group at the Centre for Molecular Informatics, part of the Department of Chemistry of the University of Cambridge.

Source code is available.
